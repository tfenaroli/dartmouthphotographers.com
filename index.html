Illuminate is a dynamic online publication aimed at creating a platform for emerging student photographers and visual artists at Dartmouth College. Published once a term by the Dartmouth Photography Society, we are dedicated to empowering young artists and fostering a community of cutting-edge creativity.

Members of the Dartmouth Photography Society were motivated to create Illuminate in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, stressing the importance of creativity and visual expression as a way to connect with one another despite our distance from campus and in direct response to this stressful, isolating time. We hope that this publication can aid in the maintenance of a strongly-tied artistic community.

If you are interested in featuring your photography in our publication, please visit our SUBMISSIONS page.
